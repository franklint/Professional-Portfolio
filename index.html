Crafting Original Playbooks for GPs

Franklin created Brimstone LLC to become the leading tactical advisory firm to provide actional advantages towards entry strategies and tactics for the private equity industry. Often, high growth categories requires the cannibalization of existing core business revenue; Franklin believes this to be a myth and holds a proven track record [circa 2009] to prove otherwise.
